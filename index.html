What is Bloomix

AN APP THAT DOESN'T PUNISH YOU FOR BEING HUMAN

Most productivity apps reset your streak when you miss a day. That guilt spiral is the #1 reason people quit. Bloomix grows a pixel plant alongside you — based on your total effort, not perfect attendance.

Your tree never dies

Go dormant, not deleted. One task brings full color back.

Milestones, not streaks

Growth is based on total active days — not consecutive ones.

Recovery over guilt

Built on self-compassion research for burnout and anxiety.

How It Works

THREE STEPS, NO PRESSURE

01
Add your daily tasks

Set tasks that work for your life — studying, job hunting, exercising, going outside. No templates or rigid structures. You decide what progress looks like for you.

Tasks reset automatically at 04:00
02
Watch your plant grow

Complete tasks and your pixel plant evolves through 5 stages over 90 days. Each milestone is permanent — your growth is archived forever in your public garden.

Growth = min(90, total_active_days)
03
Miss a day? No problem

Your plant turns grey — never deleted. It just waits. Come back whenever you're ready, complete one single task, and full color is instantly restored. No lectures, no resets.

health_min = 0, never null
